ABOUT US

Cultivating character, intellect, and creativity in young adults

Our goal for students is unwavering integrity, a desire to learn in a rigorous academic environment, and enthusiastic participation in community life.
Among private alternative schools in Jewar, Blue Bird Public School is exceptional. Located in the heart of Jewar, total enrollment 500+ and class sizes average thirty students. The Blue Bird Public School specializes in unleashing each student's potential by providing a learning environment that is safe, friendly, structured, and supportive. BBPS is ideal for students who struggle in larger public classrooms or other private schools. Whatever the issue, The School is committed to the success of each student.

OUR FACILITIES

Small School, Big Heart

Information & Communication Technology

The school has an ICT labs and all computers are equipped with multimedia kits. Technology is used to make teaching and learning meaningful and fun.

Science Laboratory

To provide hands-on practical experience to our students, excellent equipment and facilities are provided in separate laboratories for Science in accordance with the governement norms.

Kindergarten Learning Centre

Learning centers focus on selected activities that allow students to create things through hands-on activities and open end instructions.

Medical Room

The School has a medical for the students and teachers as well in case of any emergency.

Fire Extinguishers

We are taking the necessary precautions in order to prevent anything untoward from happening in the case of an emergency, like a fire. Every floor must has a fire extinguisher. These can be used to contain the fire before it spreads.

Large Classrooms

The facility of large classrooms is provided in school so as to make the learning process easier for the students.
